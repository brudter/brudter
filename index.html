About Me

"Yaakov?" you ask. "Who's that?" I'm so glad you asked. Yaakov is a very, VERY good boy from Chicago, Illinois. Yaakov loves to sit for hours and code, but even more so, Yaakov loves procrastinating, self loathing, run on sentences, and speaking in the third person. What a quirky fellow he is. "Does it count as a run on sentence if I put a bunch of commas in there?" Yaakov asks. Nobody responds. Yaakov is in his bedroom alone. "I'm pretty sure that counts as a run on sentence" he chimes to himself. Yaakov then cried himself to sleep.
